## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** Email address, name, and password when you create an account
- **Profile Information:** Phone number and other optional profile details
- **Communication Data:** Messages and interactions through WhatsApp chatbots you create

### 2.2 Information from Third-Party Services

When you connect third-party services to our application, we may collect:

#### Google Calendar Integration
- **Calendar Events:** Event titles, dates, times, descriptions, and attendees (read-only access)
- **Email Address:** Your Google account email for identification
- **Calendar Metadata:** Calendar names and settings

**We only request read-only access to your Google Calendar. We do not modify, delete, or create events on your behalf.**

### 2.3 Automatically Collected Information

- **Log Data:** IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited
- **Usage Data:** Features used, actions taken, time spent on the Service
- **Cookies:** Session cookies for authentication and preferences
